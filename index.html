What is music?

Music is an art form that consists of sound organized in time. It typically includes elements such as melody, harmony, rhythm, and timbre. Music can be created using various instruments, the human voice, or electronic means. It serves many purposes, including entertainment, emotional expression, communication, and cultural preservation. Different genres and styles of music exist across the world, reflecting the diversity of human creativity and tradition.
